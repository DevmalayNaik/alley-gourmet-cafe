Our Story

Bhubaneswar's
Hidden Aesthetic Cafe.

Located at SUM Hospital Square, Kalinga Nagar — Alley Gourmet Cafe is the cozy corner that Bhubaneswar didn't know it needed. A place for students to study, professionals to unwind, and food lovers to genuinely indulge.

We serve handcrafted specialty coffee, gourmet wraps, pasta, burgers and more — all made with quality ingredients and real passion. Add live acoustic music and free WiFi, and you've got the perfect spot for any hour of the day.
